Tournament Overview

The Event

The Pickle Yard Moneyball Throwdown is a one-day, winner-takes-it pickleball tournament. Twelve teams battle through pool play, then the top 3 from each pool cross into a single-elimination bracket. One afternoon. One champion. A thousand-dollar pot on the line.

Quick Facts

  • Teams: 12 (2 players per team)
  • Pools: 2 pools of 6
  • Pool Play: 5 games each · round robin
  • Playoffs: Top 3 from each pool
  • Prize Pool: $1,000 · paid top 3

Venue — Pickle Yard Mauldin

15 Jenkins Court, Mauldin, SC 29662 · 6 indoor courts · full-service bar & kitchen (Dilly's Oasis) · outdoor patio.

All matches played indoors on championship-grade courts. Doors open at 12:30 PM for warm-up and check-in.

Tournament Format

1

Pool Play — 5 Round Robin Games

12 teams split into Pool A and Pool B (6 teams each). Every team plays the 5 other teams in its pool once. Wins, then point differential, then head-to-head break ties.

2

Top 3 Each Pool Advance

Six teams qualify for the playoff bracket: A1, A2, A3 from Pool A and B1, B2, B3 from Pool B. The bottom three in each pool are eliminated.

3

#1 Seeds Get a Bye

A1 and B1 — the top team from each pool — skip the quarterfinals and go straight to the semifinals.

4

Cross-Play Quarterfinals

A2 vs B3, and B2 vs A3 — pool winners keep working, pool runners-up fight to join them. Two matches, two survivors.

5

Semifinals & Final

A1 takes the QF1 winner. B1 takes the QF2 winner. Losers play for 3rd place. Winners meet in the Final. Champion takes $500.

Scoring & Match Length

Pool play: Games to 15, win by 2, single game (approx. 15–18 min).

Playoffs: Best of 3 to 11, win by 2. Final: best of 3 to 15, win by 2.

"Moneyball" rule: The last point of each pool-play game is worth 2 points if the serving team wins the rally. Swings close games and rewards closers.

Frequently Asked

What skill level is this for?

Intermediate to advanced — 3.5+ DUPR recommended but not required. It's a money event, expect competitive play.

Can I sign up solo and be paired?

Email info@thepickleyard.com — we keep a solo list and pair when we can.

What if I need to cancel?

Full refund up to 7 days before the event. After that, credit toward a future Pickle Yard event.

What paddles / balls?

Bring your own paddle (any USAP-approved). Official ball: Franklin X-40 indoor.

Are spectators welcome?

Absolutely — grab a drink at Dilly's Oasis and cheer. Free entry for spectators.
