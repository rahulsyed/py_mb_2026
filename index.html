Tournament Overview

The Event

The Pickle Yard Moneyball Throwdown is a one-day pickleball showdown where 12 teams battle through pool play before the top 6 meet in a single-elimination bracket. One afternoon. $1,000 on the line. The best two players walk away with bragging rights and cash.

Quick Facts

  • Format: 2 pools of 6 · round robin
  • Games: 5 per team in pool play
  • Courts: All 6 run simultaneously
  • Playoffs: Top 3 each pool · #1 seeds get bye
  • Entry: $60 per team · Cap: 12 teams

Venue — Pickle Yard Mauldin

15 Jenkins Court, Mauldin, SC 29662 · 6 indoor championship courts · Dilly's Oasis full-service bar & kitchen · outdoor patio · free spectator entry.

Doors open at 12:30 PM for check-in and warm-up. All matches played indoors, climate-controlled.

Tournament Format

1

Pool Play — 5 Round Robin Games

12 teams split into Pool A and Pool B (6 each). Every team plays the 5 others in their pool. All 6 courts run simultaneously — 3 Pool A matches, 3 Pool B matches per round.

2

Top 3 From Each Pool Advance

A1, A2, A3 and B1, B2, B3 — six teams qualify for the bracket. Bottom 3 from each pool are eliminated. Standings: Wins → Point differential → Points-for.

3

#1 Seeds Get a Bye

A1 and B1 — pool winners — skip the quarterfinals and go straight to semifinals. They earned the break.

4

Cross-Pool Quarterfinals (Courts 1 & 2)

QF1: A2 vs B3 · Court 1  |  QF2: B2 vs A3 · Court 2. Two matches, two survivors who face the pool winners.

5

Semifinals, 3rd Place & Final (Courts 1 & 2)

SF1: A1 vs QF1 winner · SF2: B1 vs QF2 winner. Losers meet for $200 on Court 2. Winners meet in the Final on center Court 1 for the $500 championship.

⚡ The Moneyball Rule

The final rally of each pool-play game is worth 2 points if the serving team wins it. Close games flip in an instant — rewards closers, punishes the comfortable.

FAQ

What skill level is this for?
Intermediate to advanced — 3.5+ DUPR recommended but not enforced. Expect competitive play; this is a money event.
Can I sign up solo?
Email info@thepickleyard.com — Pickle Yard keeps a solo list and pairs when possible.
What's the refund/cancellation policy?
Full refund up to 7 days before the event. After that, credit toward a future Pickle Yard event.
What paddle and ball do we use?
Bring your own USAP-approved paddle. Official ball: Franklin X-40 indoor.
Are spectators welcome?
Absolutely — free entry for spectators. Grab a seat at Dilly's Oasis and cheer your people on.
How does payment work?
Register here to hold your spot. $60 per team is collected by Pickle Yard staff when you check in on event day.
